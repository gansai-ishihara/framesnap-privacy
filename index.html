FrameSnap Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Overview

FrameSnap ("the App") is a video frame extraction tool developed by Tsuyoshi Iino. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All video processing and frame extraction is performed locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data for ad personalization, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Usage data

For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your photo library solely to:

  • Select videos for frame extraction
  • Save extracted frame images to your device

Your media files are never uploaded to any server.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
